AFX MIDI


Classic Design | Modern Technology

AFX MIDI is the brain child of veteran DJ, Producer, Electrical Engineer and Software Engineer Adam Freemer. Adam has been designing and building hybrid analog / digital devices for many years and MIDI controllers since 2013. AFX MIDI was founded on the principles of building high-quality MIDI control devices with a distinct vintage DJ gear style and aesthetic. Being a fan of both vintage DJ devices and modern digital DJ software, Adam's designs attempt to bridge the gap between modern digital device design and the classic look and feel of DJ gear from times past.
